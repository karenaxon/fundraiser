Ixpaco, Santa Rosa, Guatemala

Guatemala is often referred to as "the land of eternal spring" because of its all year long spring-like weather and its diverse ecosystems. Ixpaco is situated in the southern region of the country and its rich volcanic soil makes it ideal for the cultivation of coffee, fruits and vegetables.
