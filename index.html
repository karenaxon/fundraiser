Our trip to Utah, April 2019

We flew into Salt Lake City and drove down to Las Vegas while visiting Arches, Capital Reef, Bryce Canyon and Zion national parks as well as Sand Hollow state park along the way. We went mountain biking on slick rock in Moab, horse back riding down the canyon at Bryce, UTVing at Sand Hollow and hiked at all parks. The weather was a bit unpredictable in the spring (mid 70s in Moab and snowing in Bryce) but it also kept the crowds down. It was the perfect time of the year for our family to go! Below are tips about transportation, loding, restaurants and anything else that my family found helpful. If you go, have as good of a trip as we did
