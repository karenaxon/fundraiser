Thank you!

The school district doesn't provide textbooks for the Ixpaco elementary school students and only two thirds of the families can afford to purchase them annually. Due to the pandemic that number was down close to half this year. The school principal was told to reuse last year's textbooks but with your help we ensured that all of the students have a textbook and can continue growing academically this year. You helped get 121 students a new textbook and all the classrooms got a much needed coat of paint too!
