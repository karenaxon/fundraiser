Fundraiser to Remodel Transform the School's Outhouses to Flushing Toilets

The elementary school in Ixpaco is aging and is in need of repairs. However, the school district lacks the resources to make the needed renovations. The staff, parents and students agree that remodeling the outhouses is the top priority.
Here is what I hope you can help us accomplish:

  • New! Replace the 3 outhouses toilets (because they are disintegrating) with flushing toilets!
  • New! Cover the pit latrines with reinforced concrete and use the existing dwellings to install flushing toilets.
  • New! Installation of water and drains for flushing toilets.
  • Replace the tin roof since the tin sheets have been corroded by the sulfur of the nearby lagoon and students get wet when using the outhouses in the winter.
  • Replace the doors of the outhouses with wood doors. The doors are corroded as well.
  • Modify the outhouses roof to be angled since when it rains water drains into a classroom through the window.
  • Installation of urinals for males to use.
  • Installation of sinks to encourage good hygiene.
  • Installation of electricity in the outhouses since they are dark, even darker in the winter months.

Fundraiser for Textbooks and Interior Paint for the Classrooms

The school district doesn't provide textbooks for the Ixpaco elementary school students and only two thirds of the families can afford to purchase them annually. Due to the pandemic that number was down close to half this year. The school principal was told to reuse last year's textbooks but with your help we ensured that all of the students have a textbook and can continue growing academically this year.
