To benefit the rural elementary school in Ixpaco, Guatemala

The school district doesn't provide textbooks and only about two thirds of the families can afford them annually. Due to the pandemic that number is down close to half. The school principal was told to reuse last year's textbooks. That is unacceptable! Please help me put textbooks in the hands of students so that they can grow academically this school year!

  • Details:
    • 225 students enrolled
    • 121 students need help purchasing a textbook.
    • $525 are needed to purchase the 121 textbooks.
    • $250 are needed for paint and materials. The school could use a new paint job since its roof was recently repaired (it used to rain on students). This will only happen if enough funds are collected and after the the textbooks are funded.
    • The school year started at the end of January but things move slowly in Guate and I just received this information.
